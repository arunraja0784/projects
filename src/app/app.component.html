The work-from-home policy is designed to allow eligible employees to work remotely, ensuring the continuity and productivity of business operations. Employees who can perform their work duties remotely and have received approval from their direct supervisor and the HR department are eligible for this arrangement. However, starting May 1, 2023, employees will be required to work from the office three days a week, with two days designated for remote work.
